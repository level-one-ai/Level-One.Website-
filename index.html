01 Audit Phase

Logic Trains: Deterministic Workflows

We engineer Logic Trains—deterministic workflows that execute business processes from trigger to resolution. Every prospect enters at a defined station (lead capture), travels along predetermined tracks (qualification logic), and arrives at a measurable destination (scheduled meeting, CRM entry, or automated nurture sequence).

Why "Logic Trains"? Traditional automation fails when edge cases derail execution. Logic Trains operate on tracks with decision switches—routing data to the correct next step regardless of input variation.
